Classic Spaghetti Recipe

A simple and rich italian pasta dish with a rich tomato sauce.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time:15mins

Cooking Time:30 mins

Servings:4 people

Difficulty Level:Easy-beginner-friendly

Ingredients

  • 200g dried spahgetti.
  • 2 table spoons of olive oil.
  • 1 medium onion,finely chopped.
  • 2 garlic cloves,minced.
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes.
  • 1 table spoon salt.
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper.
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves.

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot of salted water cook spaghetti for 10 mins.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan,add onion and cook till softened..
  3. Add canned tomato,salt and pepper.
  4. Allow to simmer for 15 mins.
  5. Drain spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Tips And Notes

For best flavour, use ripe canned tomatoes or fresh ones if in season. Always cook spaghetti until al dentefor the right teture. Dont forget to taste and adjust seasoning before serving!
